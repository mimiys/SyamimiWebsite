ABOUT MIMIY

Nur Syamimi Binti Che Shaiful Nazirman, or known as Mimiy was born on January 11st, 2004 at Hospital Kuala Lumpur(HKL). Mimiy is the first born of the family and she is the only daughter that Mimiy's parents have, aside from her 3 younger brothers. Eventhough both of Mimiy's parent came from different states, which is Kelantan and Pahang, yet she can barely speaks both of the dialects due to the lack of practise on speaking it in daily basis. Despite all of that, Mimiy did manage to understand both of the dialect perfectly without having any problems.
